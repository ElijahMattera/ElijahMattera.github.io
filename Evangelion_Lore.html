Evangelion Lore

Neon Genesis Evangelion is a landmark anime series blending mecha action with deep psychological and philosophical themes. The story follows Shinji Ikari and other young pilots as they battle mysterious beings called Angels using giant bio-mechanical robots known as Evangelions. The series explores identity, trauma, and the human condition, set against a backdrop of apocalyptic stakes and enigmatic organizations.

  • Shinji Ikari: The reluctant protagonist and Eva pilot.
  • Rei Ayanami: The enigmatic, quiet pilot with a mysterious origin.
  • Asuka Langley Soryu: The confident, fiery Eva pilot from Germany.
  • Gendo Ikari: Shinji's distant father and leader of NERV.
  • Angels: Powerful beings whose attacks threaten humanity's survival.

Evangelion is renowned for its complex narrative, iconic visuals, and lasting influence on anime and pop culture.
