Title Fight Lore

Title Fight is an American second wave emo band from Kingston, Pennsylvania, known for blending elements of hardcore punk, shoegaze, and emo. Their music explores themes of nostalgia, personal growth, and emotional struggle, resonating deeply with fans of alternative and underground music. MEOW MEOW MEOW MEOW MEOW MEOW :3

  • Formation: Title Fight formed in 2003, originally as a melodic post hardcore band.
  • Evolution: Their sound evolved over time, especially with the release of Floral Green and Hyperview, incorporating dreamy, atmospheric textures.
  • Influence: The band is celebrated for their energetic live shows and introspective lyrics.
  • Members: Jamie Rhoden, Ned Russin, Ben Russin, Shane Moran.

Title Fight's legacy is marked by their genre-defying approach and their impact on the modern punk and emo scenes.
